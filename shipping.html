Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

1. Digital Services

Scan & Go Edu primarily offers digital payment processing services for educational institutions. As our core services are digital in nature, traditional shipping does not apply to our main offerings.

2. Hardware Components

For educational institutions that require hardware components (such as QR code scanners or point-of-sale terminals):

  • Processing Time: Orders are typically processed within 2-3 business days.
  • Shipping Methods: We use reputable courier services for all hardware shipments.
  • Delivery Timeframes: Standard delivery within India takes 3-7 business days depending on the destination.
  • Tracking: Tracking information will be provided via email once your order ships.
  • Shipping Fees: Standard shipping is free for all educational institutions within India.

3. Educational Materials

For training materials and educational resources:

  • Digital Materials: Most educational materials are provided digitally and are available for immediate download after purchase.
  • Physical Materials: Any physical training materials will be shipped following the same policy as hardware components.

4. International Shipping

For international educational institutions:

  • We currently offer international shipping to selected countries.
  • International shipping times vary from 7-21 business days depending on the destination.
  • Additional customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

5. Address Verification

Please ensure that your shipping address is complete and accurate. We verify all addresses but cannot be responsible for delivery issues arising from incorrect information.

6. Shipping Restrictions

We only ship hardware and physical materials to verified educational institutions. A valid institution email and documentation may be required for certain shipments.

7. Lost or Damaged Items

In the event that items are lost or damaged during shipping:

  • Please notify us within 48 hours of receiving damaged items.
  • For lost items, please contact us if your package hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe.
  • We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and arrange for replacement if necessary.

8. Contact for Shipping Issues

For any questions or concerns regarding shipping, please contact our support team at shipping@scangoedu.example.com or through our Contact page.
