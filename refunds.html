Cancellation and Refunds Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

1. Digital Transactions

For transactions processed through the Scan & Go Edu platform:

  • Transaction Errors: If you believe you have been incorrectly charged, please report the issue within 48 hours of the transaction.
  • Verification Process: We will investigate all reported transaction errors and provide refunds for verified incorrect charges.
  • Refund Timeline: Refunds for verified transaction errors will be processed within 5-7 business days.

2. Subscription Services

For educational institutions with subscription plans:

  • Cancellation: You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.
  • Pro-rated Refunds: For annual subscriptions, we provide pro-rated refunds for the unused portion of your subscription period.
  • No Refunds for Monthly Plans: Monthly subscription plans cannot be refunded for the current billing month once the month has started.
  • Trial Period: If you cancel during your free trial period, no charges will be applied.

3. Hardware Products

For hardware components purchased for use with our system:

  • Return Period: Hardware may be returned within 30 days of delivery if in original packaging and unused condition.
  • Defective Items: Defective hardware will be replaced at no cost within the warranty period.
  • Refund Process: Upon receipt and inspection of returned hardware, refunds will be processed within 10 business days.
  • Shipping Costs: Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer except in cases of defective products.

4. Implementation Services

For custom implementation and integration services:

  • Project Cancellation: If you need to cancel a project before completion, charges will apply based on the percentage of work completed.
  • Satisfaction Guarantee: If implementation services do not meet the agreed-upon specifications, we will work to resolve the issues at no additional cost.
  • Refund Limitations: Custom development work that has been delivered according to specifications is not eligible for refunds.

5. Educational Workshops and Training

For training sessions and educational workshops:

  • Cancellation Timeline: Full refund if cancelled 14+ days before the event, 50% refund if cancelled 7-13 days before.
  • No Refunds: No refunds for cancellations less than 7 days before the event.
  • Rescheduling Option: As an alternative to cancellation, you may reschedule one time at no additional cost if requested at least 7 days before the event.

6. Process for Requesting Refunds

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at refunds@scangoedu.example.com or through the Contact page.
  2. Provide your order/transaction details and reason for requesting a refund.
  3. Our team will review your request and respond within 2 business days.
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase.

7. Educational Institution Accounts

For educational institutions with special arrangements:

  • Cancellation and refund policies may be modified based on individual contract terms.
  • Please refer to your specific service agreement or contact your account manager for details.

8. Contact for Refund Issues

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our refund policy, please contact our support team at refunds@scangoedu.example.com or through our Contact page.
